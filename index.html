ABOUT US

BEE Media is one of the leading up-and-coming production companies in Egypt and the wider MENA region. Founded in 2008 by two dedicated media entrepreneurs; Director Tamer Ashry and Producer Moustafa Salah.

We run a full-fledged production company that provides the standard range of high-quailty media services from Pre to Post production.

BEE has gained a reputation in the Arab World for being creative, high-quality, and professional. We specialize in the production of commercials, social campaigns, documentaries, reality TV and feature fiction films to meet the market’s needs.
